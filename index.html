About Me

Coding Is My Passion

I was born in Siantar on February 18, 2003. My interest in programming began at the age of 17. During high school, I studied Software Engineering at SMKN 1 Pematangsiantar, North Sumatra. After graduating in 2021, I pursued higher education at Universitas Islam Negeri Sumatera Utara (UINSU), majoring in Information Systems.

I have now graduated with a Bachelor of Computer Science degree, completing my studies in 3 years and 8 months. During college, I actively joined IT communities with a strong focus on programming. I also participated in the Bangkit Academy program for 6 months, specializing in Cloud Computing, and completed an internship at Dinas Kominfo Pematangsiantar for one month. In addition, I frequently worked on various website development projects and gained experience in video editing.
